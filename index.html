Summary

Paul Rand once said "Design is so simple, that's why it is so complicated"- He could not be more right. His words are what I keep in mind every time I design. Design is a method of expression for me. In every design project presented I see as a opportunity to explore and expand my skills.

When it comes to work one might say that I am a design-workaholic, I devote my time and energy into designing something that is innovative and inspiring. I am a energetic straight talker that loves working with people who are equally design driven as I am.
